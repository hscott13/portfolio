About

HEATHER SCOTT

A capable and varied creative who has experience working in both administration and the creative industry. Having graduated with First Class Honours in New Media Art, I developed my creative disciplines primarily in animation and film which is now progressing into the theatre industry. I have worked on a range of projects surrounding different mediums and industries such as projection art and Lumia. Also have experience working as a Development Worker in which I carried out multiple responsibilities such as project coordination, workshop creation and social media. I have strong digital knowledge, a creative understanding alongside adept administration talents and good communication skills.
